Face Mask Detection




The purpose of this project is to detect if a person is wearing a face mask or not. Since the model is deployed on Server, Performance issues might occur in terms of low fps.
Do try out different possibilities of face masks to give the model a run for its money!




Inspiration to build this project came from the tragic years when COVID-19 was at its peak. A simple application like this one could automate workspace/public space safety checks where face masks could automatically be checked at entry points of venues
